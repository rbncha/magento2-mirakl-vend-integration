We are pleased to announce TradeSquare and Vend have partnered to make your wholesale buying seamless, allowing you to spend less time sourcing products, and more time servicing your customers.

Our new partnership means your wholesale purchases on TradeSquare will be synced automatically into Vend's platform as soon as they're shipped!

Smarter wholesale purchasing with TradeSquare and Vend - how it works:

 

  • Make your wholesale purchases on TradeSquare.com.au
  • Once your order is shipped, the details of the product will be automatically transferred to your Vend account
  • You'll receive an email confirming your products have been synced with Vend, including links to product details.
